Who is John?

I found myself in coding.

I always thought my dream was to be a Civil Engineer. That was the only profession I know when I was in grade school. During my high school days, I always hate Mathematics and lost interest in pursuing Civil Engineering. I was doing well in high school but I think I am looking for something interesting. By that time, I am always playing games on a computer rental because I do not have my own pc.

One time, I accidentally clicked the Developer Mode in a browser and got curious about what was written in it. Having no idea what is it, I researched it and that's when it happened; the spark I was missing ignited the instant I clicked my first HTML and CSS tutorial video. By that time I decided to take a BS in Information Technology in college. Exactly because at that time I have not decided yet what course I will take. Since then, I can't get enough. Front-end web development calls to all my passions; it incorporates creativity and problem-solving.

I love applying responsive design principles and watching my web pages shrink into mobile screens and still look amazing. It's oddly satisfying. I think in a way I'm kind of like those web pages; I'm moldable, but I still keep my creative flair intact.

My specialties include quickly learning new skills and programming languages, problem-solving, responsive design principles, and web optimization. So far, I have JavaScript, HTML, CSS, C Language, jQuery, Bootstrap, ReactJS, and GitHub under my belt. I've started learning Node.JS, MongoDB, and Express.JS. I'm still enthusiastically grabbing onto any programming languages, frameworks, or principles I can integrate into the coding web in my head.
